Why to donate Blood????

Safe Blood saves lives and improve health. Blood transfusion is needed for:
1)women with complications of pragnancy,such as ectopic pagnancies and haemorrhage before,during or after childbirth;
2)Children with serve anaemia often resulting from maleria or malnutrition;
3) people are serve trauma following man-made and natural disasters;and
4) many complex medical and surgical procedure and cancer patients.


There is a constant need for regular blood supply because blood can store for only a limited time before use. Regular
blood donations by a sufficients number of healthy people are needed to ensure that safe blood will be available wherever
it is needed.


Blood is the most precious gift that anyone can give to another person- the gift of life. A Decision to donate your
blood can save a life, or even several if your blood is saperated into its components- red cells,platelets and plasma
